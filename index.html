Specialised Applications

Tailored image log analysis solutions for diverse energy sector challenges

Efficiency & ROI Optimisation

Advanced image log interpretation and fracture characterisation for unconventional and complex reservoir plays. We integrate high-resolution borehole imaging with traditional petrophysical logs to quantify fracture density and orientation, significantly improving well placement and completion designs to maximise recovery and asset value.

Production Maximisation

Supporting reservoir optimisation and production enhancement strategies for major operators through integrated formation evaluation. We leverage advanced petrophysical workflows to identify bypassed pay zones and optimise drainage patterns, ensuring precise water saturation and permeability modeling to drive sustained production growth in mature and developing fields.

Energy Transition

Specialised expertise in CCUS and net-zero initiatives, applying advanced petrophysics to ensure seal integrity and storage capacity. We evaluate reservoir injectivity and mineralogical stability to de-risk carbon sequestration and geothermal development for sustainable energy strategies.

Frontier De-risking

Expert image log interpretation for exploration projects, reducing uncertainty in strategic decisions through detailed facies and structural analysis. By characterising complex textures and tectonic features at the borehole scale, we provide the critical petrophysical constraints needed to validate exploration models and mitigate geological risk in unproven basins.
